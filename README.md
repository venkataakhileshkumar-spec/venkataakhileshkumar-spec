## 🧑‍💻 About Me

> *"Architecting enterprise-scale solutions at the intersection of healthcare and modern cloud-native technology."*

I'm a **Senior Application Architect** with **11+ years** of deep expertise in Healthcare IT, currently driving enterprise-scale solutions at **Health Care Service Corporation (HCSC)**. Based in **McKinney, TX**, I specialize in Data Engineering, Microsoft SQL Server, Power BI, Azure Cloud, and application architecture — with a career trajectory that reflects consistent upward growth from Programmer Analyst to Senior Application Architect.

My work spans complex payer-side healthcare systems and modern cloud-native architecture — covering member eligibility, claims processing, ETL pipelines, and platform modernization. With a **Master's in Electrical Engineering** and over **11 years of industry experience**, I bring both the technical depth and domain authority to lead mission-critical healthcare initiatives from the ground up.

- 🔭 **Currently working on:** Enterprise-scale healthcare data platforms at HCSC
- 🌱 **Currently mastering:** Azure Data Lake advanced architectures & ML integrations
- 📝 **I write on:** [Medium](https://medium.com/@venkataakhileshkumar) — technical articles on Data Engineering & BI
- 🎓 **Research publications:** [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2nPVNSQAAAAJ&hl=en)
- 💬 **Ask me about:** SQL Server, SSIS, SSRS, SSAS, Power BI, Azure Data Factory, ETL Design
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I debug T-SQL queries faster than most people Google the syntax!
- 🏥 **Domain expertise:** Healthcare payer systems — claims, eligibility, and benefits processing
- 🌍 **Location:** McKinney, TX | Open to remote opportunities
